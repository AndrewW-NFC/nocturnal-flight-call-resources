NFC acoustic environment forecast

Browser-based tool that scores hourly weather data for sound clarity and propagation—the acoustic environment for nocturnal recording. It does not forecast bird activity or migration. Fetches public weather and twilight data, generates scores and downloadable reports, all in your browser.
